Some Functions of the Laity Councils

A Catholic Parish Laity Council, also known as a Pastoral Council, is a consultative body that advises the parish priest or pastor on matters related to the spiritual growth and development of the parish. The functions of a Catholic Parish Laity Council include:

  • Advising the pastor: The council provides input and advice on parish matters, such as pastoral planning, budgeting, and community engagement.
  • Fostering community: The council helps build a sense of community among parishioners, promoting unity and cooperation.
  • Developing parish programs: The council helps plan and implement programs for spiritual growth, education, and social justice.

As members of our church family, we are all called to work together to build God's kingdom. As the body of Christ, we are all interconnected, and the laity council is an integral part of our church's ministry. By supporting them, we are supporting the growth and well-being of our entire church community. Let us rally behind our laity council, praying for them, attending meetings, and offering our talents and resources. Together, we can achieve great things for God's glory! Remember, every single one of us has a vital role to play in the life of our church. Let us celebrate our unity and work together to further God's mission.

Organizations

The Men, Women, and Youth Organizations serve as vital pillars of community engagement, spiritual growth, and service.

C.M.O.N.

Calling all gentlemen of faith!

The Catholic Men Organization plays a vital role in fostering a community of faith, service, and fraternity among men within the Church.

By empowering men to live out their faith with courage and integrity, the organization contributes to the building of a stronger, more vibrant Catholic community grounded in love, compassion, and solidarity.

C.W.O.

The Catholic Women Organization is a vibrant and integral part of the Catholic community, comprising women who are dedicated to serving their faith, their families, and their communities. Founded on the principles of faith, service, and sisterhood, the C.W.O. provides a platform for women to come together in fellowship, prayer, and action.

The Organization stands as a testament to the power of women's faith, dedication, and service in building a better world guided by the principles of love, compassion, and justice.

Join Learn More

C.Y.O.N.

The Catholic Youth Organization (C.Y.O.N.) is a dynamic and vibrant organization within the Catholic Church that aims to engage young people in their faith, empower them to live as disciples of Jesus Christ, and inspire them to become leaders in their communities.

Overall, the Catholic Youth Organization plays a vital role in nurturing the faith, leadership, and service of young Catholics, empowering them to live out their faith authentically and to be agents of positive change in the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I get involved in parish ministries or volunteer opportunities?

    Many parishioners seek ways to actively participate in the life of the parish, whether through liturgical ministries, outreach programs, or community events.

  • What religious education programs are available for children and adults?

    Families often inquire about options for religious education and formation, including classes for children preparing for sacraments and ongoing faith formation opportunities for adults.

  • How can I request sacraments such as Baptism, Marriage, or Anointing of the Sick?

    Parishioners may have questions about the process for arranging sacraments for themselves or their loved ones, including requirements and scheduling.

  • What support services does the parish offer for those in need?

    Individuals facing challenges such as illness, bereavement, or financial hardship may seek information about support services available through the parish community.

  • Are there opportunities for spiritual growth and formation available for adults?

    Individuals may be interested in adult faith formation programs, retreats, or study groups to deepen their understanding of the Catholic faith and grow in their relationship with God.

  • How can I support the parish financially or contribute to specific projects or ministries?

    Parishioners may inquire about options for giving financially to support the ongoing mission and ministries of the parish, as well as specific fundraising efforts or capital campaigns.
